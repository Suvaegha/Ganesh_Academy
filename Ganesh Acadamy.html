About us

We are the no.1 south Indian language training institute with an experience of more than 2 decades for majority of Indian languages and all south Indian languages with a mission to make all Indians multilingual.

As of now we have our 1 main center in Chennai where we take up all classes, meeting and other ops and we do have 2 other optional/partnership centers which we can use whenever we want and planning to expand even more in forth coming months.

Mainly we take language classes for adults and corporates in all possible mediums. Apart from language classes we also take up tutoring session for school and college students. We have our own team of experts from all regions to help in our path.
